An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software that companies use to screen resumes before a human ever sees them. It parses your resume, extracts information, and scores it against the job requirements. If your resume uses incompatible formatting, missing keywords, or non-standard headers, the ATS may score it low — and your application gets filtered out automatically. Most large companies and many mid-sized ones use ATS software.

Most tools just count keyword matches. ResumeATS goes deeper — it analyzes formatting and parsability (tables, columns, and graphics that ATS systems choke on), section structure, contact info placement, content quality, action verb strength, and achievement quantification. Then it delivers a full rewrite, not just a list of words to add.

We support PDF and DOCX files up to 10MB. PDF is recommended — it gives the most accurate parse and is the most common format. The AI-rewritten resume is delivered as a clean plain-text download that you can paste into any word processor or submit directly.
